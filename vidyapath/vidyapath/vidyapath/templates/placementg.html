Placement Process

Step 1: Registration

Students register for placement activities through the college portal.

Step 2: Pre-Placement Talks

Companies visit the campus to provide details about their requirements and job roles.

Step 3: Aptitude Tests

Students take aptitude tests as part of the screening process.

Step 4: Technical Interviews

Shortlisted candidates undergo technical interviews.

Step 5: HR Interviews

Final interviews are conducted to evaluate cultural fit and communication skills.

Step 6: Offer Letters

Successful candidates receive offer letters from the companies.

Banasthali Vidyapith Strictly Follows the 'One Student One Job Policy'

The Placement opportunity is made available to all the students eligible and registered for placement , strictly following the policy of "One Student One Job."

Once a student is selected by a company, she will not be allowed to participate in any other campus drives organized . The sole objective is to ensure that every student gets equal job opportunities and that only a few students do not consume all the jobs.

Banasthali Vidyapith Alumni have been consistently performing well in the placement opportunities given to them and had helped us strengthen our corporate associations year on year. Due to our outstanding Alumni Achievers working in corporates, our fresher students get good mentoring and guidance when they enter the corporate world.

Every student who clears the selection process with a company should join and work in the same company for an initial 2-3 years, eventually enhancing her skills, network, certification, and qualification.

If a student has been recruited by a company, the concerned student will not be allowed to appear for any other interview for placement arranged . However, placed students may be considered to sit for other drives on visits of Companies offering higher packages or a core stream company.

Strict Discipline

Strict discipline needs to be followed by every student during the recruitment process.

Any students found misbehaving or during misconduct will be debarred from the entire placement season.

No casual talks or remarks should be extended by any students during the process or even after the process to company delegates.

Students cannot forgo any selection round post the registration, that is conducted by the companies they have registered for. This includes PPT, Online Test, GDs, interviews etc. Failure in abiding by the above would have strict actions taken by College Authorities.
